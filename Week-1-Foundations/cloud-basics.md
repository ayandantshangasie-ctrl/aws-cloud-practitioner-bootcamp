Cloud computing is the use of the internet to store, manage, and access data or computer services instead of using only a personal computer or hard drive. It allows people and businesses to use things like storage, software, and servers online from anywhere as long as there is an internet connection.           
(a)IaaS(Infrastructure as a Service) – The company gives you the basic computer infrastructure like servers, storage, and networking online, and you manage the rest yourself. It’s like renting an empty house and furnishing it your own way.                                          (b)PaaS (Platform as a Service) – The company provides both the infrastructure and tools needed to build applications, so you mainly focus on creating and running your app. It’s like renting a fully equipped kitchen where you only focus on cooking.                                                                   (c) SaaS (Software as a Service) – The company provides ready-to-use software online, and you simply use it without worrying about maintenance or setup. It’s like eating at a restaurant where everything is already prepared for you.
